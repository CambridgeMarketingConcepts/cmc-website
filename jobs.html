  • Review code and output of engineering peers

  • Familiarity of containerization and deployment

Back-End Developer

Job Description

  • Work collaboratively and efficiently across functional teams in a fast-paced startup-like environment.
  • High standard of engineering quality through code reviews and unit tests.
  • Extend our platform to handle an expanding set of features.
  • Design, extend, and implement APIs.
  • Proactively work with DevOps to solve system problems.

  • Strong attention to detail

UX/UI Designer

Job Description

  • Take broad, conceptual ideas and turn them into something useful and valuable for our internal users.
  • Design easy-to-understand end-to-end flows and use the appropriate prototyping tools to demonstrate how a particular flow or interaction will work.
  • Give and solicit feedback from other designers to continually raise our bar for quality.
  • Partner with Product Managers, engineers, and content strategists to oversee the user experience of a product from conception until launch.

Requirements

  • 2+ years of experience designing and delivering products from start to finish as part of a cross-functional team.
  • Deep understanding of user-centered design methods and best practices.
  • Experience in strategic product thinking with the ability to understand product goals, identify opportunities and make decisions based on the impact to people and the company.
  • Interaction design experience with ability to define how an experience should behave based on understanding people's needs.
  • Visual design experience with proficiency in typography, desktop/mobile UI, color, layout, iconography, and aesthetic sense.
  • Technical knowledge of front-end, back-end and APIs.

Compensation & Benefits
